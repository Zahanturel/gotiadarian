Preserving Ancient Revelations

For centuries, the Goti Adarian has stood as a sacred sanctuary where the eternal flame burns bright, connecting our community to the profound teachings of Prophet Zarathustra. Here, tradition meets modernity, and ancient wisdom guides contemporary life.

Our fire temple serves not just as a place of worship, but as a cultural cornerstone that preserves the rich heritage of the Parsi-Zoroastrian community. Through prayer, ceremony, and community gathering, we maintain the sacred traditions passed down through generations.
