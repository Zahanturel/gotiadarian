Community Guidelines

Visiting the Temple

  • Dress modestly and respectfully when visiting
  • Remove shoes before entering prayer areas
  • Maintain silence during prayer services
  • Follow guidance from temple priests and volunteers

Community Participation

  • All community members are welcome to participate
  • Volunteer opportunities are available for all ages
  • Registration required for some special events
